## Compact Yet Powerful
The amaran Halo 60x is an excellent choice for creators needing a reliable, portable key light. At 63W with bi-color range (2,700K - 6,500K), it delivers versatile color temperature control for matching any environment—from warm indoor setups to cool daylight conditions.

## Bowens Mount Compatibility
Like other quality lighting solutions, the Halo 60x features a standard Bowens mount. This opens up endless modifier options—softboxes, lanterns, reflectors—without being locked into proprietary gear.

## App Control & Portability
Control brightness and color temperature directly from your phone via the companion app. The ultra-compact form factor makes it perfect for on-location shoots, fitting easily into travel kits without sacrificing output quality.

## Great for Content Creation
Whether you're shooting YouTube videos, vlogs, or photography sessions, the Halo 60x provides studio-quality lighting in a package small enough to throw in a backpack.
