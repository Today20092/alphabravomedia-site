My primary camera for almost all client work. The FX3 combines cinema camera features with mirrorless portability.

## Why I Use It

- S-Cinetone color science
- Incredible autofocus for solo shooting
- Compact and gimbal-friendly
- 4K 120fps for beautiful slow motion

## Best For

Corporate videos, documentaries, and any shoot where you need reliability and beautiful images without a large crew.
