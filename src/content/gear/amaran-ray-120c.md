## Full-Color RGB Control
The Ray 120c is a powerful 120W RGB panel light offering extreme creative flexibility. With a color range spanning 1,800K to 20,000K, you can dial in virtually any color temperature or creative color effect imaginable.

## IP54 Weather-Resistant Design
Built tough for professional use. The IP54 rating means it can handle humidity and some spray, making it suitable for various shooting environments beyond the studio.

## Portable Yet Powerful
Despite its 120W output, the Ray 120c remains genuinely portable with included softbox and carrying case. Perfect for productions where you need serious output without the footprint of traditional studio lights.

## App Control & Bowens Mount
Full app control for wireless operation combined with a standard Bowens mount for modifier compatibility. The inclusion of a softbox makes it ready to use right out of the box.

This is a solid choice for anyone serious about video production. Highly recommended for ambitious creators and production teams.
