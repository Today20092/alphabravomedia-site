## Travel-Friendly Power
I use this while traveling because it keeps my NP-FZ100 batteries organized and ready without taking up much space in my kit.

## Faster USB-C Charging
The USB-C PD support makes this a much faster charging setup than the usual slow charger, which is a big help when I’m moving between shoots.

## Charge Batteries Individually
The NP-FZ100 batteries I pair with this setup also have USB-C ports, so I can charge each battery on its own without the case if I want.

## Practical For Production Days
It is a simple, useful piece of gear that fits the way I work. Compact, reliable, and easy to throw in a travel bag. Tilta also makes similar charging setups for other camera systems like Canon, Lumix, and Nikon, which makes the lineup easier to understand if you shoot more than one brand.

## Recommended
If you shoot Sony and want a more flexible charging setup for travel, this is a solid pick.
