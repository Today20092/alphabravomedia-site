The Sony FX3 continues to be the backbone of my production workflow. In this video, I break down why it's still my go-to camera for client work.

## What Makes It Special

The FX3 hits a sweet spot between cinema camera capabilities and mirrorless versatility. You get S-Cinetone out of the box, incredible autofocus, and a form factor that doesn't scream "production crew" on smaller shoots.

## The Good

- **Autofocus**: Best-in-class eye tracking that just works
- **S-Cinetone**: Beautiful skin tones without heavy grading
- **Form Factor**: Compact enough for gimbal work
- **4K 120fps**: Buttery smooth slow motion

## The Considerations

It's not perfect for everyone. If you need raw recording or higher frame rates at full resolution, you might want to look at the FX6 or Venice lineup.

## Final Thoughts

For run-and-gun corporate work and content creation, nothing else in this price range comes close.
