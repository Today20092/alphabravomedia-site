## 1. What We Collect

Alpha Bravo Media only collects the information needed to respond to inquiries, plan projects, deliver services, and maintain normal business records. This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, event details, and project requirements when you contact us directly.

## 2. How Information Is Used

We use contact and project information to:

- reply to inquiries
- prepare estimates and service recommendations
- coordinate scheduling, production, and delivery
- maintain internal business records

We do not sell your personal information.

## 3. Contact Channels and Embedded Services

This website may link to or embed third-party services such as YouTube, Google Maps, Google Reviews, social platforms, or a hosted contact form provider. Those services may collect technical usage data according to their own privacy policies.

## 4. Cookies and Analytics

This site may use standard browser storage or cookies that support site functionality, preferences, and embedded media behavior. If analytics or form providers are enabled, they may also set their own cookies.

## 5. Data Retention

Inquiry and client records are kept only as long as reasonably necessary for communication, project delivery, bookkeeping, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

## 6. Your Rights

If you want to update, correct, or request deletion of personal information you previously shared with Alpha Bravo Media, contact us directly and we will handle the request in a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

## 7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to [contact@alphabravomedia.co](mailto:contact@alphabravomedia.co).
