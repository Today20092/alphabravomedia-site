## 1. Booking & Payments

To lock in and secure your date, Alpha Bravo Media requires a **25% non-refundable retainer (booking fee)**. This ensures that we turn away other potential clients for your specific date and time. Your booking is not considered official until this retainer is received and the contract is signed.

The remaining balance for your project is due **14 days prior** to the event or production start date.

## 2. Cancellations & Rescheduling

We understand that unexpected circumstances arise. If you need to reschedule or cancel, we require written notice at least **14 days prior** to the scheduled date.

- If canceled within 14 days of the event, the client is responsible for 50% of the total remaining balance.
- In the event of rescheduling, the original 25% retainer can be transferred to a new date (subject to our availability) if notice is provided at least 14 days in advance. If we are unavailable on your new date, the retainer remains non-refundable.

## 3. Creative Control & Revisions

We pride ourselves on our cinematic storytelling, and we value your feedback. To ensure a smooth post-production process, we include the following structured revision rounds in our standard packages:

- **Short-form Content (Under 6-7 minutes):** Up to **three (3)** rounds of revisions.
- **Long-form Content (Over 7 minutes, documentaries):** Up to **two (2)** rounds of revisions.

Any additional revision rounds beyond these limits will be billed at an hourly editing rate. Revisions should be submitted compiled in a single list format. A "revision" constitutes minor adjustments to pacing, music, or specific clips—it does not cover a complete structural re-edit of the project.

## 4. Raw Files, Delivery & Archiving

Our completed, color-graded, and sound-mixed films are the final deliverable. However, we understand clients may want untouched archival footage.

- **Raw Footage:** Full, unedited raw footage is available for an additional fee. To receive raw footage, the client must supply a physical hard drive (USB 3.0 or better) with sufficient storage capacity.
- **Archiving:** We keep final project files and edited footage archived for **60 days** after final delivery. We highly recommend downloading and backing up your final files immediately upon receipt. Alpha Bravo Media is not liable for data loss after this 60-day period.

## 5. Venue & Halal Guidelines

We are committed to providing services in a completely halal manner that respects our community's values. At this time, we are exclusively offering videography for the **men's section and non-segregated general events**. Until we can bring a dedicated female videographer onto our team to respectfully capture the women's side, we will strictly adhere to these guidelines.

For events requiring our Audio/PA services or live projection, it is the client's responsibility to ensure the venue permits us to tap into their soundboards, set up lighting equipment, and utilize staging areas.

By booking with us, you grant Alpha Bravo Media the irrevocable right to use recorded media in our portfolio, website, and social media for standard promotional purposes, unless a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is negotiated and signed prior to the booking.

## 8. A/V Setup & Logistics

For audio and live projection services, the Client is responsible for securing adequate space for equipment setup and ensuring necessary access to the venue at least 2 hours prior to the event start time. ## 9. Delivery Timeline

We prioritize a high-quality post-production process. Final deliverables will be provided within **3 to 8 weeks** following the event date, depending on the complexity of the project and our current editing queue.

## 10. Travel & Parking

Travel up to 45 minutes from the Tampa, FL area (including Hillsborough County, Pasco County, and surrounding areas) is included. Travel beyond this radius may incur additional mileage or travel fees, quoted prior to booking.

The Client is responsible for any applicable parking fees, valet costs, or venue access fees required for the Alpha Bravo Media team to provide services on site.

## 11. Force Majeure & Severe Illness

We will make every reasonable effort to find a suitable replacement videographer for your event if such an emergency occurs.

## 12. Non-Exclusive Coverage & Interference

While we will work professionally alongside others, Alpha Bravo Media is not responsible for missed moments caused by the physical interference or obstruction of other individuals.

The safety of our team and equipment is paramount.
