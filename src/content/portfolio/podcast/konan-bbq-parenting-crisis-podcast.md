## Full-Service Podcast Production for Konan BBQ

For this Konan BBQ podcast episode, Alpha Bravo Media handled the full production workflow behind the release, helping the client turn a recorded conversation into a polished YouTube-ready podcast asset.

The episode, titled *Kids Get A's But Can't Read? The Parenting Crisis in Black Education*, continues the Konan BBQ podcast's focus on community conversations, real-life lessons, and issues that matter to families and business owners in the Tampa Bay area.

## Production Role

Alpha Bravo Media supported the episode from capture through publishing preparation. The production included broadcast-style audio recording, post-production cleanup, metadata support, and social media deliverables designed to help the episode reach more viewers after release.

Key production work included:

- Recording with Shure MV7+ microphones
- Audio engineering for clear, consistent dialogue
- EQ and compression to shape the voices and control dynamics
- Audio mastering for a polished final sound
- YouTube title support
- YouTube description writing
- Chapter marker creation
- Thumbnail creation
- Full social media production planning
- Social media reels prepared to help promote the podcast and drive views back to the full episode

## Audio Engineering and Mastering

The goal was to make the hosts and guests sound clean, present, and professional without distracting from the conversation. Using Shure MV7+ microphones gave the recording a strong foundation, while post-production EQ, compression, and mastering helped smooth out levels, improve clarity, and make the final episode feel ready for public release.

## YouTube Packaging and Social Distribution

Beyond the edit itself, Alpha Bravo Media also helped package the episode for YouTube. That included the title, description, chapter markers, and thumbnail so the upload had a stronger presentation and was easier for viewers to navigate.

The episode will also be supported with social media reels, giving Konan BBQ short-form content that can be posted across platforms to boost discovery and bring more viewers back to the full podcast.
