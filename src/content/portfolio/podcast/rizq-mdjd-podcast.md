## Full-Service Podcast Production for Rizq MDJD

Rizq MDJD is a podcast focused on Muslim financial empowerment, wealth-building, and practical conversations around rizq, mindset, and stewardship. The show brings together in-person discussions and, more recently, remote guest episodes that expand the conversation beyond the table.

My role is to handle the entire production workflow from start to finish so the hosts can stay focused on the conversation. That includes:

- Bringing and setting up the camera, lights, audio equipment, and recording gear
- Recording and managing the video and audio during the session
- Editing the full episode and tightening the pacing
- Writing the episode description
- Uploading the finished video to YouTube
- Creating short-form reels from the episode as an optional add-on for clients who want more content from each recording
- Uploading those reels to YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, or any other platform they want

For the newer episodes with online guests, I also manage the technical side of bringing the remote guest into the conversation and cutting between the guest and the hosts cleanly so the episode still feels polished and intentional.

The goal is to make the process simple for the client while keeping the final result professional. Instead of worrying about gear, setup, file management, or post-production, they can just show up and have the conversation.

The short-form clips are especially valuable because they help pull attention back to the full episode and give the podcast more chances to reach new viewers across different platforms.
