## Capturing Your Timeless Memories: Introducing Our Wedding Services

Over the past few months, we have had the honor of hearing from many of our amazing clients who love our video work, asking us the same question: *"Do you do weddings?"* 

Because of this incredible word-of-mouth support, we are thrilled to officially announce that we are bringing our cinematic expertise to wedding videography! We are now offering premium, halal wedding videography services specifically tailored to the Muslim community here in Tampa Bay and the surrounding areas.

## Our Cinematic Style & Approach
Your wedding day is a celebration of love, family, and beautiful traditions. We specialize in a blend of cinematic and documentary-style storytelling. We don’t just record your event; we capture the true vibe, the joy, and the environment so you can relive those lasting memories for years to come. 

Every wedding project we deliver features:
*   **High-Quality 4K Cinematic Footage**
*   **Professional Color Grading**
*   **Custom Sound Design**
*   **A Beautifully Crafted Highlight Film:** Tailored to reflect your unique story and emotions.

*(Note: Raw footage of your event is also available for an additional fee, provided you supply a USB drive with sufficient storage capacity).*

## Important Note on Halal Coverage
We are committed to providing services in a completely halal manner that respects our community's values. **At this time, we are exclusively offering videography for the men's section and non-segregated general events.** Until we can bring a dedicated female videographer onto our team to respectfully capture the women's side, we will strictly adhere to these guidelines.

## Premium Audio & Live Display Setup (Add-On Service)
Beyond just capturing the video, we know how critical crisp audio and visibility are for a successful event—especially during speeches or the Nikkah. As an exclusive add-on to our videography packages, we offer full A/V event support:
*   Professional speaker setups and audio mixing.
*   Wireless microphones to ensure crystal-clear audio for the Imam and speeches (no venue echo or feedback).
*   Live camera feeds wirelessly projected onto venue screens or projectors, so every guest has a front-row view of the ceremony.
