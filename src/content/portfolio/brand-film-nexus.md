Created a cinematic brand film showcasing Nexus Tech's innovative approach to sustainable technology.

## The Brief

Nexus needed a hero video for their website rebrand that would communicate their mission of making sustainable tech accessible to everyone.

## Approach

We shot over two days at their headquarters and manufacturing facility. The goal was to juxtapose the human element (the passionate team) with the precision of their technology.

## Technical Details

- Shot on Sony FX3 with Sigma Art primes
- Lit with Aputure 600d and 300x panels
- Graded with custom S-Cinetone base
- Delivered in 4K HDR and SDR versions

## Results

The video has been viewed over 50,000 times and helped Nexus close their Series B funding round.
