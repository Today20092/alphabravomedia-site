Directed and shot music video for indie artist Aurora's single "Midnight."

## Concept

Aurora wanted something ethereal and dreamlike that matched the moody, introspective nature of the track. We developed a concept around the idea of being lost between waking and sleeping.

## Production

Shot guerrilla-style over one night in downtown locations. The limited crew (just 4 of us) allowed for mobility and spontaneity.

## Visual Style

- Heavy use of practical lighting (neon signs, street lights)
- Slow motion at 120fps for the chorus
- Anamorphic lens flares for that cinematic feel
- Desaturated grade with teal/orange color contrast

## Impact

The video helped Aurora land a sync placement on a major streaming series and grow her monthly listeners by 40%.
