An intimate look at three master woodworkers keeping traditional craftsmanship alive in the modern age.

## Why This Project

In a world of mass production and disposable goods, I wanted to spotlight the makers who still do things the old way—with patience, skill, and pride.

## The Subjects

- **James Morrison** - 40 years building custom furniture
- **Elena Vasquez** - Hand-carved wooden sculptures
- **Robert Chen** - Traditional Japanese joinery

## Filming Approach

We spent a full day with each craftsman, observing their process and conducting sit-down interviews. The goal was to capture the meditative quality of their work.

## Festival Run

The documentary was selected for three regional film festivals and won "Best Short Documentary" at the Pacific Northwest Film Awards.
