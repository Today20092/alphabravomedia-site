# Alpha Bravo Media — Brand Color Palette

> Premium & Cinematic + Warm & Personal
> Font: Sora (headings) + Inter (body)

---

## Color Tokens

### Primary Accent — Copper
- **Hex:** `#c8875c`
- **RGB:** 200, 135, 92
- **Use:** CTAs, buttons, links, active states, key highlights
- **Psychology:** Warmth, craftsmanship, golden hour, premium quality

### Secondary Accent — Sage Green
- **Hex:** `#7c956b`
- **RGB:** 124, 149, 107
- **Use:** Secondary buttons, tags, badges, subtle highlights, section accents
- **Psychology:** Authenticity, organic, grounding, personal touch

### Highlight — Warm Cream
- **Hex:** `#f5e6d3`
- **RGB:** 245, 230, 211
- **Use:** Special text highlights, hover states, featured labels (alternative to pure white)
- **Psychology:** Soft warmth, intentionality, elegance

### Body Text — Warm Gray
- **Hex:** `#a8998a`
- **RGB:** 168, 153, 138
- **Use:** Body/paragraph text, descriptions, secondary content
- **Psychology:** Cohesive warmth, readable, approachable

### Headings / Primary Text — White
- **Hex:** `#ffffff`
- **Use:** H1-H3 headings, nav links, primary labels

### Muted Text — Dim Warm Gray
- **Hex:** `#7a6f63`
- **RGB:** 122, 111, 99
- **Use:** Dates, captions, metadata, tertiary info

### Background — Near Black
- **Hex:** `#0b0b0b`
- **Use:** Page background, primary surfaces

### Background Alt — Soft Black
- **Hex:** `#0e0e0e`
- **Use:** Alternating sections for depth variation

### Surface — Dark Card
- **Hex:** `#141211` (warm-tinted dark)
- **RGB:** 20, 18, 17
- **Use:** Cards, panels, elevated surfaces

### Border — Warm Border
- **Hex:** `#2a2520` (warm-tinted border)
- **RGB:** 42, 37, 32
- **Use:** Card borders, dividers, section separators

## Usage Guidelines

- **Copper** is the hero color — use it for the most important action on any page
- **Sage green** supports copper — never let it compete; use for secondary actions and accents
- **Warm cream** replaces pure white sparingly for a softer, more branded feel
- **Warm grays** replace Tailwind's cold `gray-*` scale for body text
- Dark backgrounds stay near-black — the warmth comes from the accents, not the background
- Use the copper glow gradient for featured cards, recommended badges, and hero elements
